How To Use

White Tomato helps you to employ the Pomodoro Technique without overwhelming options.

Usage:

  1. Click "Start" to start a 25-minute timer.
  2. Focus on your work with the help of the white noise background.
  3. Take a break once the white noise stops.
  4. Click "Reset", and repeat from Step One.

Tips:

  • Lower the volume. Aim for the lowest that is still effective for helping you focus.
  • You can choose between white, pink, and brown noise under "Options".
  • Complete a simple physical task during your break, like washing your coffee cup. Your body will thank you for that.
  • Don't spend your time overthinking how to be more productive. All you need is here. Hit "Start" and work.
